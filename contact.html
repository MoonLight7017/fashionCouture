Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a custom outfit?

Typically, a custom outfit takes 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity and your requirements. We can also offer express services for urgent orders.

Do you offer alterations for existing garments?

Yes, we provide professional alteration services for all types of garments. We can adjust sizes, modify styles, and make repairs as needed.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. You can cancel or reschedule appointments up to 24 hours in advance without any charges.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we serve customers within India. We're working on expanding our services to international markets soon.

Can I bring my own fabric?

Absolutely! We welcome customers who want to use their own fabric. Our designers can guide you on the best way to use your chosen material.

How many fittings are included?

Our standard package includes 2 fittings, but we offer unlimited fittings in our premium packages to ensure perfect results.
